📖 Setup Definitions
🏦 Inside Job Failed Reclaim
Price attempts to reclaim an important level and fails to hold acceptance, trapping participants on the wrong side and creating a momentum reversal.
💵 Loose Cash Gap Fill
Price targets an inefficiency or gap left behind from a prior session or news event. Easy money sitting on the table — price goes back to collect it.
💥 Vault Breach Liquidity Sweep
External liquidity is raided — price takes out obvious stops above highs or below lows — then reverses or expands in the opposite direction.
📋 Blueprint Hit HTF Level Reclaim
Price reacts from a preplanned higher timeframe location and follows the expected path. The plan was written before the session — execution is just pulling the trigger.
🚗 Getaway Route Range Expansion
Compression resolves and price accepts into expansion. A coiled market breaks out of its range and runs — you're in the car before the doors close.
🎭 Unmarked Score Custom
A setup that doesn't fit a standard classification. Describe it manually. Use sparingly — if you're writing this often, it might be a new setup worth naming.
